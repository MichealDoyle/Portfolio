Photo Restoration of Damaged Photo

The goal of this project was to repair the damage done to an older photo, as well as to enhance the overall quality of the photo and the faces of those in it while retaining its original details. The damages needing repaired included tears, scratches, and creases.

Tools Used

  • Spot healing
  • Neural filters
  • Clone stamp tool
  • Adjustment layers
  • Smart filters

Skills Displayed

  • Photo enhancement
  • photo reconstruction
  • Curves and levels adjustment
  • Black and white adjustment
  • Tear, scratch, and crease removal

Reflection

This project helped me understand how to use non-destructive editing to restore a badly damaged photo while keeping most of the original details. It also helped me get a better uderstanding of using neural filters while maintaining the time period aesthetic.
