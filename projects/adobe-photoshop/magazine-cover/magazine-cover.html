Digital Magazine Cover

This project required us to make a quality digital magazine cover of a fictional or non-fictional individual from scratch. The goal was to gain more experience and become more comfortable working with the tools in photoshop while applying elements of design such as the layout, visual composition, and typography.

Tools Used

  • Healing Brush
  • Crop
  • Text
  • Shape
  • Layer Effects

Skills Displayed

  • Typography
  • Visual Composition
  • Spacing
  • Color Scheme
  • Creativity

Reflection

The outcome of the project is vibrant, organized, and spacious design that very clearly displays the theme to the best of its ability. The choice of font along with the color scheme gives great contrast while the composition of the layout makes it easy to focus on the things you are reading.
